Tips

Here are some general fitness tips for you.

  • Calories

    A pound is equal to 3,500 calories. Remember to burn more calories than you consume to lose weight!

  • Your Diet And Exercise Need To Co-Exist

    It is not enough to only exercise. Without a proper diet, losing weight may prove to be a challenge.

  • Eat Smaller Portions

    Eating smaller portions may help you control your intake of food.

  • Consider Eating More Vegetables

    Vegetables provide necessary vitamins, and minerals necessary for good health.

  • Make Subgoals

    Having a long term plan may be overwhelming. To make sure you are on track, consider breaking down your goals into weekly or daily goals.

  • Sleep, Sleep, Sleep!

    A lack of sleep can make you hungry, weak and tired. The daily recommended hours are seven to eight hours each night.

  • Stick To A Routine

    A routine can motivate you to make sure you reach your goals.

  • Workout With A Friend or loved one

    Reach your goals with someone you comfortable. This can help you keep motivated.

  • Don't Be Too Hard On Yourself

    Set backs happen often, Don't be discouraged. Don't Give up and continue to pursue your goals. Learn from your mistakes.

  • Give Up Sugar

    Sugar often times promote over-eating. A person may not be aware of the amount of calories they are consuming. Not only may sugar promote over-eating. Consuming too much sugar can lead to many other illnesses. It is recommended that you be aware of your consumption.

  • Stay Hydrated

    Drink Water! Water is very important. Most adults need around two quarts of fluid per day to replace normal water loss from daily activities.
